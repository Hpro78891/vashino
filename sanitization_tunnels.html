Sanitization Tunnels

Mass Sanitizing Tunnel is the need of the hour to combat the Corona virus disease (COVID-19) which is a highly contagious and is declared pandemic by most of the countries as well as WHO. COVID-19 has been declared a Public Health Emergency of International Concern (PHEIC) and the virus has now spread too many countries and territories. While a lot is still unknown about the virus that causes COVID-19, we do know that it is transmitted through direct contact with respiratory droplets of an infected person (generated through coughing and sneezing). Individuals can also be infected from touching surfaces contaminated with the virus and touching their face (e.g., eyes, nose, and mouth). While COVID-19 continues to spread it is important that communities take action to prevent further transmission, reduce the impacts of the outbreak and support control measures. We have come up with an innovative concept of the Mass Sanitizing Tunnel. This is the single Most Effective solution for Disincentive public gathering / crowded places like Hospitals, Malls, Railway Stations, Bus Station, Banks, Government Offices,Army Establishments, Factories, Office Buildings, Etc. The innovative system has many advantages, highlighting the drastic reduction in the consumption of water and disinfectant, up to 5 times compared to a traditional spray disinfection system. Moreover, fogging guarantees a uniform distribution of the disinfectant / sanitizing agent across the Human body and product may have to be treated at the same time minimizing moisture ingress in the high-risk zone. To further reduce the consumption, modules incorporate an automatic start system when they detect a person and automatic shutdown after a programmable time without person Standard Features: • Structure Made of Painted M.S. Rectangular Pipe. • Easy to assemble at site due to Easy mobility. • Due to mobility, it reduces manpower. • Level Indication in Sanitizer tank. • For batter Sanitizing of the body there is use of Ultrasonic Fogging Unit. • Using WHO approved Sanitizer for Fogging. • HDPE Sheet to cover all the structure. • Photocell on discharge, stops the Fogging in the absence of Human Scope of supply In hospital entry gates Railway platform Airport checking point Mall ,cinemas entry Companies entry gates At congested gates. How it Works ? A person walks in, and places their hands under the sanitizer pump towards their left side, they use the sanitizer to disinfect his hand. Next the sensors in the tunnel start the fogging of the sanitizing mix. Within 30 seconds the whole process of preventing the virus is over. It prevents the virus from spreading not only on your body, but also from any items you might have on you. If the tunnel is empty for more than 30 seconds then the sensor stops the fogging process. Until the next person walks in where the whole process starts again. The product can be tailored according to your requirements. The larger sizes are for factories, transport stations, intersections
